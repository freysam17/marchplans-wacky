Risk Mitigation

Identified risks and strategies to address them.

Volunteer No-Shows
Medium Prob Medium Impact
+
Confirm attendance 48 hours AND 24 hours before shoot. Recruit 10 volunteers knowing 2–3 may drop. Have in-house team ready to cover critical roles. Core production (camera, sound, lighting) is handled internally regardless.
Location Falls Through
Low-Med Prob High Impact
+
Confirm locations in writing (email or WhatsApp) with clear date/time. Have 1 backup shortlet and 1 backup restaurant identified. Pay deposit or sign simple agreement where possible. Scout backup public locations that could work in emergency.
Talent Issues
Low Prob High Impact
+
Lock talent with deposit payment. Confirm availability multiple times. Have contact info for 1–2 backup actors who auditioned. Keep Day 1 scenes flexible enough to reorder if needed.
Film Underperforms
Medium Prob Medium Impact
+
Paid ads guarantee baseline reach regardless of organic performance. Direct outreach does not depend on views. Film still serves as portfolio piece for all future pitches. Learn and improve for Episode 2.
No Commercial Production Leads
Medium Prob Low-Med Impact
+
Editing and podcast services continue generating revenue regardless. Film elevates all pitches even if it doesn't directly convert in March. Episode 2 compounds the effect. The series builds credibility over time.
